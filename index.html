About the studio

Studio-KDC

Studio-KDC (Kartik Design Collective) is an architecture and design practice focused on creating climate-responsive, context-driven spaces.

Our work blends modern design thinking with local traditions, materials, and sustainable principles. We believe architecture should be timeless, honest, and rooted in its surroundings.

Each project is approached with clarity, restraint, and a deep respect for place, people, and purpose.
